Linguine al pesto

Description

Typical genoan dishes used in every part of Italy. Long lenghted pasta with a basil and pine nuts sauce.

Ingredients
  • linguine pasta
  • basil
  • pine nuts
  • parmiggiano
Steps
  1. Put basil in a mixer for 30 mins
  2. Add pine nuts and parmesan to the sauce
  3. Boil the water
  4. After the water is boiling, put the pasta in
  5. let the pasta cook for 12 mins
  6. put the pasta and the sauce in the same plate and eat it
