Lasagna

Description

Lasagna's are an institution, typical dishes for a sunday with your family. It can come in different versions, but you can stay sure that there will always be layers of deliciousness.

Ingredients:
  • 350g of durum wheat
  • 150g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300g meat
  • 50g carrots
  • 100g red wine
  • 300g tomatoes
Steps
  1. Prepare the sauce
  2. let the sauce cook for 8hrs
  3. add the meat to the sauce
  4. Serve the sauce between the browse and then serve the plate.
