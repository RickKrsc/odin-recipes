Sorrentinian gnocchi

Description

One of the most known campanian dishes in the world. Gnocchi surrounded of a veil of tomatoes and parmiggiano and mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Gnocchi
  • Basil 6 leaf
  • tomato sauce 600g
  • mozzarella 250g
  • parmiggiano 70g

Steps

  1. Cook the tomato sauce
  2. boil the water for 5 mins
  3. put the gnocchi in the water for 15min
  4. mix everything and eat it
